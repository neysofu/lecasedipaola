"I tre camini" vacation rental in Villa del Ferro (Vicenza)

Bed linen and towels are changed every three days, cleaning is carried out every day. If you ask in advance the owner can organise a time saving route for a visit to the Villas. Information is available on horse-riding, golf courses, night concerts in the Villas, local festivals, exhibitions, wine & food itineraries, wine cellars and antique markets. 

For stays including more than three nights the owner puts at the disposal of guests her large kitchen, where guests can cook their own meals. Domestic help is supplied upon request (price not included in the rate). If this solution is chosen you can ask the owner to buy what you need in advance and you will find the provisions when you arrive (price not included in the rate).
